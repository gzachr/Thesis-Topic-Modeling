Creamy Milk Flan
10 eggs
separate egg yolk from albumen
1 can of evaporated milk
1 can of condensed milk
stir the mixture until evenly blended
1  can of condensed milk
drain until there's no air bubbles
in  a small aliminum  container pan 
melt white sugar over heat
wait until it's caramelized
Vanilla syrup
pour 1 teaspoon of vanilla syrup
cover with aluminum foil
Steam over boiling water for 20- 30 minutes
Ready to Serve!!
