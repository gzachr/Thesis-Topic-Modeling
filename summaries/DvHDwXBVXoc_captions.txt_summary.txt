Did you know that Davao city is home to mount Oppo the highest peak in the Philippines. Davao crocodile Park is a hub for wildlife conservation and offers a thrilling experience for adventure Seekers. Museo dabawenyo is a government-funded attraction showcasing the Region's Rich history and culture. Rojas Avenue Night Market is a vibrant atmosphere filled with enticing Aromas and Lively conversations. Eden Nature Park is a fascinating destination for outdoor enthusiasts and ecotourists. One of the top destinations that should not be missed is samal Island. The tropical area offers a range of activities for every type of traveler. As night falls you can enjoy an adventure-filled evening Under The Starry Sky.
